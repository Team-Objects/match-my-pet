Greg

Greg is a 7 year old orange tabby cat. He has been with Katharine since he was born. In his younger days he was man about town, roaming freely around outdoors during the day. After one too many fights and bird kills, he has since retired from the outdoor life. After a recent move, Greg has upgraded to a home with large windows all around. While his outdoor time is now limited to an enclosed cat patio.

Tippy,Blanca,Harold,Bentley,Tenchi

Tippy is the loudest and youngest of the gang that takes care of Vida and she runs a tight ship with regard to securing the pantry. Blanca and Harold are level-headed middle-children with Blanca's serious intelligence tempered by Harold's good-natured goofiness. Bentley retired from a tech career in Seattle to the Carolinas to spend his golden years giving in whole-heartedly to his anxiety triggers and loudly demanding that his designated human stop all productive activity promptly at 9:00 PM (or he'll only get louder). Tenchi was the bestest kitty in the whole world and was Vida's rock for 21 years; sadly he passed away a few months ago and awaits her while frolicking at the rainbow bridge.
