Welcome to Match my Pet, a fun matching game where you can see cute pictures of our pets and learn a little bit more about them and us.


To play, enter your user name and select a difficulty level: easy, medium, or hard. Each level allows you a limited number of turns. The harder the level, the fewer opportunities you have to get a correct match.
